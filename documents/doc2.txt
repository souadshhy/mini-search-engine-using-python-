Artificial intelligence is a broad area of computer science that tries to create systems capable of tasks that normally require human intelligence. These tasks can include recognizing speech, classifying images, planning routes, or answering questions. Many people use the words artificial intelligence and machine learning together, but they are not exactly the same. Machine learning is one approach inside artificial intelligence. A useful way to think about it is that artificial intelligence is the larger field, while machine learning is a practical method for learning from data. In real applications, AI systems still need careful design, testing, and human supervision.
