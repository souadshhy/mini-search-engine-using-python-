Cloud computing allows users to access computing resources such as servers, storage, and databases over the internet. Instead of buying and maintaining physical machines, organizations can rent resources when they need them. This flexibility is useful for web applications, data analysis, and machine learning experiments. Cloud systems often provide managed databases, monitoring dashboards, and deployment tools. However, cloud computing also requires careful cost management and security planning. A poorly configured service can become expensive or expose sensitive data, so developers must understand the platform they are using.
