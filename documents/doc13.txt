Cybersecurity protects systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access and misuse. A good cybersecurity plan includes authentication, access control, encryption, monitoring, and user awareness. Search technology can also help in security work. For example, analysts may search log files to find suspicious activity, repeated errors, or unusual access patterns. Information retrieval techniques are useful because security logs can become very large. Indexing the logs makes investigation faster, especially when analysts need to search by IP address, username, error message, or timestamp.
